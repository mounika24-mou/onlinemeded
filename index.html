The OnlineMedEd curriculum uses the power of a blended learning paradigm, with
each piece serving a very specific purpose. PACE (Prime, Acquire, Challenge,
Enforce) is designed to emphasize understanding and retention, making your
learning more efficient and effective. You get a better grasp of the material now
and remember it later when it counts.
